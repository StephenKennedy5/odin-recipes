Asian Noodles

Description

Ingredients

    Sauce

  • 3 table spoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1.5 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons chili garlic sauce, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 3 tablespoons creamy peanut butter

    • Noodles

  • 2 (3 once) packages ramen noodles
  • 2 tablespoons chopped peanuts
  • 2 medium green onions, thinly sliced diagonally

Steps

  1. Whisk soy sauce, sesame oil, brown sugar, rice vinegar. chili garlic sauce, and ginger together in small bowl.
  2. Add peanut butter, whisking until well combined, set aside.
  3. Bring 4 cups of water to a boil in a pot.
  4. Discard flavor packet and add ramen noodles to the boiling water.
  5. Cook until noodles are tender, 4 to 5 minutes.
  6. Drain noodles, reserving some noodle water in case you need to thin out sauce later.
  7. Pour sauce over ramen noodles, tossing until well coated. If sauce too thick, thin out with small amount reserved noodle water until reach your desired consistency
  8. Hot noodles wil soak up the sauce. Garnish with peanutes and green onion, and serve!
