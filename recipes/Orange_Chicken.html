Orange Chicken Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 Boneless Skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
  • 1.5 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 3 green oinions, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste

Steps

  1. Place the chicken and cornstach into a bag or bowl, and toast to coat.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or larget skillet over medium-high meat.
  3. Fry the chicken pieces and garlic, stirring until lightly browned.
  4. Stir in soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and water.
  5. Cover and cook until the chicken pieces are no longer pink inside, 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Stir green onion, cayenne pepper, cook uncovered for about 2 more minutes.
  7. Serve over white rice.
